Who I Am

Hello, beautiful people of the Internet! My name is Angel, and I'm a tenth grade student at Summit Shasta High School. (To any person and/or alien that may be reading this in the future, this was written in 2015, and Summit Shasta is a high school with non-existent walls and no chairs.) I'm just a really nerdy kid that has way too much time on their hands but never knows what to do with it. If you ever checked in on me during my free time, you'd most likely find me just scrolling through my phone, laughing at funny Instagram posts, checking out some of my favorite artists, or doodling funny-looking cartoons.

So yeah. The things that I do are pretty simple, but I don't think that I could really count them as hobbies. And having almost no extracurricular activities doesn't give me much of an idea for what I want to be when I grow up. If you asked me what I wanted to do in the future, it's most likely that I'd just stroke my chin and mutter something "wise" and "profound" right before sprinting away. And of course, when I say "sprinting", I mean jogging. Very very slowly. Almost at a walking speed. Because you know, physical exertion is just ... just no.

But I do have to admit that although I have no long term goals in mind, all I want to do is make things that make people happy. If my work makes me smile and laugh, then I want to make other people smile and laugh ... and maybe make them pee their pants. Definitely make them pee their pants.
