How to Use the Calculator
Just in case some of you have never touched a calculator before (or if mine just looks really weird), here's what you got to know: All that you have to do is choose two numbers. One of them goes into the first box, and the other goes into the second box. Then just choose whatever operation you want to be done between them and click the "Calculate" button for your answer.
